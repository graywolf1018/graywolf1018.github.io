Image process                                                                         

It’s my PH.D. study, mostly is about CCD problem. CCD is color sensors in the camera, for cost down, it usually only one for each pixel, that is the famous Bayer Color Filter Array.

But for each pixel, we should have 3 color to have a full image. How to interpolate the image is one of my main research.

In my research we use lots of ways to solve this problem, include frequency domain analysis, algebra, detection and estimation…etc.

After all, we use non-linear interpolation way to design the best algorithm to solve this problem and publish on IEEE.

Tools：C++, MATLAB

Autobiography

My name is Young, I graduate at PH.D. of communication engineering at National Chiao-Tung University.

I’ve skipped the master degree and study for the PH.D. directly since I was the first-place award of our school project (when I was 3rd grade in college) and have an IEEE Imaging Systems and Techniques paper (First author) when I was 4th grade in college. My professor thought I have nice potential at research so recommend me to directly study the PH.D. instead to have a Master degree first. I though it’s a nice opportunity and I really like to do the research, so I decide to do that.

But I knew, PH.D. always been discussing with not close enough with the company, so I aggressive to apply the internship of some nice company in Taiwan, like Mediatek, TSMC.

When I in Mediatek as an intern engineer, my manager recommends me to attend a national IOT competition, so start to have experimented with firmware design, electronic resign and control, and also, I have very nice performed at the competition. So, I have lots of opportunity to be invited to the more IOT discussion and competition.

After that, I meet lots of famous veteran or great manager and also lots of founder of startups. I knew that I can’t waste my lives drawing lines, so not only technical, I also study about knowledge of the business, marketing and PM…etc. 

I attend my fir startup company which named ARSENZ in 2014, the main product is smart glass which can use on security and medical area.

After the ARSENZ, I startup with some of my friends, we start a new business named “ALCHEMA” which is an IOT home appliances. It can be brewing the wine at home just as easy as make coffee. It just needs to use the device with the instruction with our APP, then all is done.

Since it’s a startup company, and I am the co-founder and CTO, lots of things I need to do by myself. Like ID design, schematic, layout, molding, plastic injection, APP design, even buying, shipping and also assembling, just as many you can image, I have all study and done them before. So, I almost learn everything about how an idea from a thought becomes a reality product.

We have lunched the “Kickstarter” on 2016 and shipping the product in 2018. We raised about 34K US dollar and more than 20 countries and 300 news are interviewed us. Like ELLE, GQ, TechCrunch.

After leaving the Alchema, I worked for Mediatek 5G team. I learn a lot of new wireless communication technology like 5G. I have joined a lot of projects which I list in the front section, I’ll now spend too much paragraph here.

Since my experiment, I think I can almost fit any kind of role in any company, and I wish I can learn even more and become one of the top managers in the world.
